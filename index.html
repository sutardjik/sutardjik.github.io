.calm ↗

An intentional visual archive of personal aesthetic memory—created to counteract the hyperstimulated logic of conventional platforms. Inspired by the philosophy of the slow web, this digital space favors presence over performance. It explores interface minimalism and attention ethics, inviting contemplative digital engagement.

Welcome to my corner of the web! ❊

I’m Karen, a self-taught strategic designer from San Francisco, blending design thinking, systems thinking, and strategy to shape meaningful, end-to-end user experiences — from systems architecture to human experience, and everything in between.

I combine a background in Computer Science (B.E. 2024) with a deep focus on creativity, accessibility, and functionality to build ethically conscious digital systems. My practice bridges design and human behavior, driven by curiosity for impact, belief in human potential, and a desire for meaningful connection.

When I’m not dreaming up designs, you’ll find me exploring fine art museums, immersing myself in cinematic indie pop, or meditating on critical theory in quiet green spaces.
