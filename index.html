Selected Works ✺

.calm ↗

A personal aesthetics gallery inspired by the minimalist style of VSCO and the visual appeal of Pinterest, challenging the toxic nature of conventional social media platforms through advocacy for the slow web movement. This digital space invites exploration and appreciation of curated aesthetics, encouraging mindful interaction and emphasizing quality over quantity in the digital realm.

PanPop ↗

An interactive population trends visualizer of six countries from 1950 to 2020 through population pyramids and annual population growth line graphs to explore population data and understand demographic changes over time. Interactive sliders can be manipulated to view data for specific years, with data sources linked to PopulationPyramid.net for additional information and context.




Welcome to my slice of the web! ❊

I’m Karen, a self-taught strategic designer from San Francisco, dedicated to merging design thinking with strategy to create impactful user experiences from initial concept to launch.

I combine a background in Computer Science (B.E. 2024) with a deep focus on creativity, accessibility, and functionality to shape ethically conscious digital spaces. My practice bridges design with human behavior, reflecting curiosity for impact, belief in human potential, and a drive for meaningful connection.

When I’m not dreaming up designs, you can find me exploring fine art museums, immersing myself in cinematic indie pop, or meditating on critical theory in quiet green spaces.
