Featured Works

Switchblade ↗

A self-written poem marking an introductory dive into the poetic realm is generated through animation using the Typed.js library. The typing cursor style subtly echoes that of ChatGPT, while the color scheme switches from light to dark mode after the completion of the stanzas. The narrated events find a parallel in real life, adding a captivating layer to the poem’s narrative.

.calm ↗

A personal aesthetics gallery that draws inspiration from the minimalist styles of VSCO and the visual appeal of Pinterest to challenge the toxic nature of conventional social media platforms by advocating for the slow web movement. This digital space is meant for exploring and appreciating curated aesthetics, encouraging mindful interaction and emphasizing quality over quantity in the digital realm.

Ascension ↗

An interactive lyric visualizer—a creative coding website—that showcases the impact of diction in song lyrics through varied typography choices. It is centered around the lyrics of the vocal piano ballad “Ascension” by Sarah Kinsley. A featured interactive still-life sakura garden showcases carefully chosen emojis to enhance the symbolism, emphasizing the deeper meaning of the song.

QuasiRealSync ↗

A data replication tool that seamlessly captures and parses binary logs from MySQL, facilitating efficient transfer to target PostgreSQL databases for replication. This tool supports CRUD operations and ensures a smooth and reliable replication process, guaranteeing data integrity while minimizing downtime.

PanPop ↗

An interactive population trends visualizer of six countries from 1950 to 2020 through population pyramids and annual population growth line graphs, engaging users in an informative way to explore population data and understand demographic changes over time. Users can manipulate interactive sliders to view data for specific years and access data sources linked to PopulationPyramid.net for additional information and context.
